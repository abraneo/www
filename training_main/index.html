Training Overview

We are a Jedox Training partner and can help you with any level of training. Whether you’re a long time customer or a prospective customer, Abraneo offers a training course that will meet your needs. We make quality training convenient. Our classes can be attended at our training facility, onsite at your location, or online at your convenience.

  • Basic Jedox ETL
  • Introduction to Jedox Web
  • Jedox for Excel JumpStart Training!
  • Introduction to Jedox for Excel
  • Advanced Jedox for Excel

Introduction to Jedox for Excel

This introductory class is designed to get you started with Jedox for Excel. Intermediate knowledge of Excel is recommended for best results. Classes can be held onsite at customer or at the Abraneo Training Center.

Upon successful completion, you will learn:
  • The difference between OLAP and relational databases
  • How to build a Jedox Cube, Dimension, Herarchy and Attributes
  • The basics of cube design
  • Input data methods using Jedox for Excel
  • How to create Ad-hoc and standard reports in Excel

Contact us for the training schedule.


Introduction to Jedox Web

This introductory class is designed to leverage your existing Jedox for Excel knowledge and gain a basic understanding of Jedox Web. Classes can be held onsite at customer or at the Abraneo Training Center.

Upon successful completion, you will learn:
  • How to interact with files and folders on Jedox Web
  • The basics of Jedox Web Spreadsheets
  • Advanced Jedox Web report writing with Jedox DynaRanges(tm)
  • Create dynamic reports using interactive spreadsheets (Combo-Box, Check-Box, Buttons, etc.)
  • Create input templates via Jedox Web
  • Administration basics of Jedox Web

Contact us for the training schedule.


Jedox for Excel Jump-Start Training! (TM)

This two-day workshop is intended to help customers and prospective customers quickly understand the core features and functionality of Jedox’ Premium Jedox with Excel.The material is presented in a fast-paced environment with “hands on” exercises to re-enforce key concepts. Intermediate (or greater) knowledge of Excel is recommended for best results. Upon successful completion, customers will be able to build dimensions, cubes, load data, and create ad hoc and standard reporting using Jedox with Excel. Workshops can be held onsite at customer or at the Abraneo Training Center.

Contact us for the training schedule.



Advanced Jedox for Excel

This advanced class is designed to further your knowledge of dimension building, cube creation, attributes, available Jedox Premium functions, advanced report writing, and gain a basic understanding of Jedox Premium rules and security. Classes can be held onsite at customer or at the Abraneo Training Center.

Upon successful completion, you will learn:
  • The power of all the Jedox Premium functions available
  • Advanced and dynamic Excel writing techniques with Jedox Premium
  • The basics of writing Jedox Premium Rules
  • Administration of Jedox Premium Security with Excel

Contact us for the training schedule.



Basic Jedox ETL

This advanced class is focused on learning Jedox ETL (Extract, Transform, and Load tool). Previous experience with ETL tools is helpful but not required. Class can be held onsite at customer or at the Abraneo Training Center.

Upon successful completion, you will learn:
  • How to create an ETL project
  • Navigate in the ETL project explorer
  • Upload a project
  • Execute a job
  • Connect to a relational database

Contact us for the training schedule.
